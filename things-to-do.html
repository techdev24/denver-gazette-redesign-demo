Your guide to what’s happening in Denver

Looking for things to do in Denver this weekend or this week? The Denver Gazette tracks live events across the metro so you don’t have to dig through a dozen ticket sites. We pull together concerts at Red Rocks Amphitheatre, Mission Ballroom, the Ogden and the Bluebird; Colorado Rockies baseball at Coors Field and other pro and college sports; theater, dance and comedy at the Denver Center for the Performing Arts and venues across the city; plus family-friendly festivals, fairs and exhibitions.

The list above refreshes daily and always shows the next seven days, so whether you’re planning a date night, a night out with friends, or a weekend with the kids, you’ll find something happening near you. Tap a category to narrow things down, then click any event for tickets and details.
